# Faith Orientation

**Faith orientation**

Some resources grant players a faith orientation.

A player may also, of course, assign themselves one at card creation.

Generally, characters who gain a faith orientation from a resource do so through Lord God exchange or some special ability.

Barring the most exceptional circumstances, every being can hold only one faith orientation; gaining a new one voids every ability tied to the previous.

Some common faith orientations:

**Christianity:**
The faith orientation under the real world's Christian systems — Protestantism, Catholicism, and the rest all fall here.

**Islam:**
The faith orientation under the real world's Islamic systems.

**Daoism:**
The faith orientation under the real world's Daoist systems.

**Buddhism:**
The faith orientation under the real world's Buddhist systems, Tibetan esoteric Buddhism and other branches included.

Certain works carry particular faith orientations not grounded in the real world. Either way, barring the most exceptional circumstances, a being holds only one faith orientation.
