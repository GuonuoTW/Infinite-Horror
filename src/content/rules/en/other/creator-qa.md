# Creator Q&A

This page carries rules Q&A between the maintainer and the players — detailed points the rules may not spell out can find their answers here.

Q: What's the difference between "counts as" and "gains"?
A: Essentially none — but note that effects granted by "counts as" apply *only* within the scope of the item in question. E.g.: counting as having infinite Breach cannot be converted through other effects into an infinite attack-check bonus.

Q: In Infinite campaigns, should card-building aim at each character having their own strengths, or at raw power?
A: Generally I recommend whatever's fun — but the former usually turns out more fun, so I recommend the former.

Q: Can I pick a special species at card creation?
A: Ordinarily a character's starting species should be human, but with the ST's leave a special species may be chosen — the PC should gain no benefit from the species.

Q: What is an "intuition action"?
A: A leftover from unrepaired legacy resources — generally treat it as a Reflex Action.

Q: What should a newcomer who wants to write their own resources do?
A: Personally I advise newcomers against self-writing; but if you must, consult the Authoring Rules and follow them point by point.

Q: Can I declare a guard in advance, before combat begins?
A: Ordinarily no — outside combat, a character shouldn't be guarding against enemies who don't exist.

Q: Negative-status immunity doesn't grant immunity to the status points — so does multiple-attack-penalty immunity block multiple-attack-penalty points?
A: The multiple-attack penalty isn't a Negative Status point; immunity to it rightly means taking no effect from the multiple-attack penalty whatsoever.

Q: An Opportunity Roll's 1 is a critical failure — with Wind-Bearer 9, does an Opp. Roll of 1 count as a success?
A: Still a critical failure; a natural 1 on an Opportunity Roll is untouched by Luck bonuses.

Q: After condensing True Origin Force, what happens to abilities that computed off the old Energy Pool?
Example: range becomes (Dao Force pool maximum × sword-immortal layers) meters.
A: Compute from the original pool's size as before — but that maximum will never change again.

Q: Suppose I'm carrying a water vat, and I have perfect flight while in water — can I soak in the vat and fly around holding it overhead?
A: Obviously not. You merely move freely within a body of water *as though* you had perfect flight — you don't actually have it.

Q: How do I exchange for an ability with no listed price?
A: Look up the matching price in the Authoring Rules by Tier and class; if neither exists, it cannot be bought or used — report the bug to the rulebook author.

Q: If an A-Tier ability dealing Poison damage or Mental damage doesn't state a Tier for the damage, can a D-Tier mind immunity or toxin immunity block it?
A: Generally the damage's source is annotated at the matching Tier — an ability dealing A-Tier Poison damage counts as an A-Tier toxin effect, and so is not blocked.
