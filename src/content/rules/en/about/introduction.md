# Introduction

The setting of the Infinite Horror TRPG rules is based on the novel of the same name, *Infinite Horror* (《无限恐怖》). Exceptional freedom is this ruleset's greatest draw: you can absolutely wield inner force and magic at the same time, commanding a flying sword from a hundred li away with one hand while hurling fireballs at your enemies with the other — or pilot a Gundam and pull off the Shōryū-Ha, or even rebuild yourself into a Transformer and raise an AT Field on top of it. None of this is out of reach.

You can also carry these formidable powers into the game, film, and anime worlds you know best — fighting their story characters, befriending them, even pursuing deeper relationships, so long as you have the ability. In the late game you may cross swords with other champions who hold the same advantages you do. If any of this appeals to you, give this ruleset a try.
