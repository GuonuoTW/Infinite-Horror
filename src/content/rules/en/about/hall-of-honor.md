# Hall of Honor

Note: the rulebook you are reading now is a derivative continuation of the 2nd edition, maintained by Guangre-jun (光热君). The 2nd edition led by Xingguang Juemie (星光绝灭) stopped updating at version 2.35 and moved on to a new 3rd edition. If you need the 3rd-edition rules, join QQ group 301122594.

The content below this point is the original text from before Guangre-jun took over. It is preserved as-is and no longer updated.

This page gives a proper introduction to the authors of this rulebook — both the writers of the rules and the writers of resources.

Zuofeihu ("the Fox"): a very kind and gentle ST who loves acting cute, and is a lolicon; even when taking leave, never flakes on a session. Adores Touhou resources and writes whenever inspiration strikes — over 100 resources submitted so far. Also loves studying our country's mythology and bits of history, writes scripts in spare time, owns a large stable of original settings — more than 20 large scripts of 300–500k characters — and "can't run a campaign without a script". Signature style as an ST: basic roleplay required, no chasing combat power, huge XP payouts, very stingy with branches and points. Handles the item section of resources; not much of a card-builder personally, and deeply allergic to bait-and-switch resources.

Representative works: Desolate Ancient Sacred Body, Silent Warlock, Five-Element Immortal Spirit Method, Golden Crow, Source, Divine Source, Dragon Marrow, the Dark-Gold Lockpicking Set, the Fox Scarf, and others.

**Infinite Universe Season One has successfully concluded! Onto the wall of honor!**

Infinite Universe Season One has wrapped. The process didn't fully satisfy me — some of it my fault, some Old Deer's, and the general feedback was that the experience wasn't great — but as an experiment in PVP it was still a success. It exposed a great many problems, and those problems are unquestionably valuable for refining the rules.
Our thanks to every PC who stuck it out through the team battles. Thank you for your support.

Across games 3–8 — six horror films and three team battles in total — Team A (Zimu-01, Shenxu, Super-Cute-Giant-Adorable-Invincible-Beautiful Little Princess, Gugu Jingjie, Lü Lin, Cute-yet-Charming Villainous Chaotic-Evil Passerby, Roma) came through three close-run team battles (two of which, frankly, I deliberately stacked against them) to win, becoming the champion team of Infinite Universe Season One. Let's all congratulate them.

Best PCs of this Infinite Horror campaign (best combat & best roleplay):

Best combat: Team A's Shenxu — carried over 80% of Team A's damage output and invented the cheeky "only I can hit you, you can't hit me" playstyle.

Best roleplay: Team B's Ma Qiandi — who without hesitation danced the "pachi-pachi, I am the pachi-est" dance, drawing such frenzied heckling from teammates that the collected heckling energy broke through a story bottleneck.
