# Authoring Rules

Special: opening a skill tree also allows opening same-Tier exclusive items.

\=====================================================================

Bloodline template:

Each rank provides Stats (D/C/B/A/AA/S = 4/6/8/12/18/24; per-Stat cap 2/3/4/6/6/8, type is Intrinsic Bonus)

\*The D rank may open an Energy Pool; having no energy adds 1 trait or 1 Stat point.

\*Each rank gains one trait that opens the same-Tier skill tree; if there is no skill tree, gain a trait or 1/2/3/4/4/5 Stat points instead.

Price: D+600 C+1200 B+3600 A+7200 AA+10800 S+14400

D rank: base 2 traits.

C rank: base 2 traits.

B rank: base 3 traits.

A rank: base 3 traits.

AA rank: base 5 traits. (A-Tier traits)

S rank: base 3 traits.

A one-time Bloodline gains 2 same-Tier traits.

One Bloodline may not hold an AA rank and an S rank at once.

Titles:

\*Titles may be leveled up with XP, but the first rank must be bought as usual.

\*Titles are advised to carry certain prerequisites.

\*The D rank may open an Energy Pool; having no energy adds 1 trait.

\*Each rank must open the same-Tier skill tree; if none is opened, merge or convert it into Arts.

\*If the Title excludes other Titles (not a recommended design), each rank adds 1 trait.

\*One-time Titles fall under Schools.

Cultivation:

Arts class

Skills / Techniques / Arts

\*If you meet an Art's conditions, it may be bought with XP.

Price: D+500 C+1000 B+2000 A+4000 S+8000

D-Tier: base 3 traits.

C-Tier: base 3 traits.

B-Tier: base 3 traits.

A-Tier: base 3 traits.

S-Tier: base 3 traits.

Doubling the points adds 1 trait.

Doubling the Tiers adds 2 traits. (may double at most once)

If it is a skill under a non-universal exclusive skill tree, add 1 more trait.

Bonuses provided by skills/Techniques/Arts default to Morale Bonuses, Reinforcement Bonuses, or Technique Bonuses.

Spells

Price: D+500 C+1000 B+2000 A+4000 S+8000

\*If you meet a spell's conditions (such as the matching caster level), it may be bought with XP.

Spells have a Power value.

D-Tier: Power 2, base 3 traits.

C-Tier: Power 4, base 3 traits.

B-Tier: Power 6, base 3 traits.

A-Tier: Power 10, base 3 traits.

S-Tier: Power 16, base 3 traits.

Doubling the points adds 1 trait.

If it is a spell under a non-universal exclusive skill tree, add 1 more trait.

Bonuses provided by spells default to Morale Bonuses, Reinforcement Bonuses, or Energy Bonuses.

Items:

Price: 1 10 200 500 D+500 C+1000 B+2000 A+4000 S+8000

1: generally an accessory (like ammunition), or 1 no-Tier trait.

10: base 2 no-Tier traits; weapons gain 1 extra trait.

200: base 3 no-Tier traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait.

500: base 5 no-Tier traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait.

D-Tier: base 5 D traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait, shields gain 1.5 extra traits, of which 0.5 must buy weapon damage.

C-Tier: base 5 C traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait, shields gain 1.5 extra traits, of which 0.5 must buy weapon damage.

B-Tier: base 5 B traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait, shields gain 1.5 extra traits, of which 0.5 must buy weapon damage.

A-Tier: base 5 A traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait, shields gain 1.5 extra traits, of which 0.5 must buy weapon damage.

S-Tier: base 5 S traits; weapons gain 2 extra traits, armor gains 1 extra trait, shields gain 1.5 extra traits, of which 0.5 must buy weapon damage.

Doubling the points adds 1 trait.

Doubling the Tiers adds 1.5 traits. (may double at most once)

\*Items upgradable by paying the price difference: traits −1.

\*Items occupying no equipment slot: traits −1.

\*Consumables double their traits (double after all additions and subtractions).
