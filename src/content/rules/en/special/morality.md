# Morality

**Morality**

A character's Morality at creation is 7. The number is a grade, not a running tally: a character's Morality may fall to 5 through constant thieving, but no amount of thieving alone drags it below 5.

When a character does something that violates their Morality grade, they roll dice per their Morality (Morality 7 rolls 4 D10s, for instance). On a success, remorse holds them at their current Morality; on a failure, the character "falls", and Morality drops one grade.

After a failed roll and the drop, roll again at the new, lowered Morality (fallen to 6, roll 6 D10s); if that roll fails, the character develops a mental disorder.

The ST picks the fitting mental disorder from the list's triggers, according to the scene at hand.

Thereafter, whenever the character is provoked, the matching disorder flares.

The ordinary person's Morality is 7 — it stands for "one shouldn't hurt people, nor take what's theirs; though sometimes the speed limit really does feel too low."
