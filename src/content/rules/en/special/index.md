# Special Rules

The content here consists of more expansive, optional rules — all of it opt-in.

Of these, Morality with Mental Disorders, the Hyperbolic Time Chamber (see Special Exchanges), and the self-taught ability rules are recommended on; the healing/reset, revival, and respec parts of Special Exchanges are better tuned freely to the ST's style of running games; and Transcendent Divinity, Creating Lifeforms, and the Crafting Rules deserve careful thought before opening.

The Authoring Rules provide templates for players writing their own resources — best attempted only once you know this rulebook well. If you'd like to see your written resources in this book, join the rulebook QQ group and submit them.
