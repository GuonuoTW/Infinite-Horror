# Feats Overview

**Feats overview:**

Feats are special techniques a character possesses. They cannot be summed up by Stats and Skills alone, yet they differ from abilities exchanged with the Lord God — they are earned through the character's own discipline and pursuit.

Learning a Feat costs:

3 XP for a level-1 Feat,

6 XP for level 2,

8 XP for level 3,

12 XP for level 4,

20 XP for level 5.

If a Feat has several levels, each level must be learned separately and in order.

**A five-level Feat spanning Feat points 1–5 therefore takes 3+6+8+12+20 = 49 XP to master completely.**

If a Feat begins directly at a higher level, simply pay that level's XP to acquire it —

so a two-level Feat spanning points 2–3 takes 6+8 = 14 XP.

For Feats whose basic prerequisite is tied to a Stat, the Feat's level may not exceed that Stat's value — someone with EDR 3 cannot have Diehard 5. If a Feat has two key Stats, the lower one sets the cap.

For combat Feats, your choice must fit your background: if your Special Identity says you're a fencing-club member, you have no grounds to take the "Master Sniper" Feat.

Any bonus or penalty in a Feat left undescribed is a Feat Bonus or Feat Penalty.
