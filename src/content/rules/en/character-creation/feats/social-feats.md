# Social Feats

===

**Insight into Hearts (1–3):**

Prerequisites: PCT 3, Empathy skill 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Empathy-related checks.

Level 2:

Once per day, when unhappy with an Empathy (Reading People) related check's result, you may reroll it — but you must accept the new result, even if worse.

Level 3:

The character gains a 3-point Insight Bonus on defense.

===

**Rousing Morale (1–3):**

Prerequisites: DMR 3, any Expression sub-skill at level 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Expression-related checks.

Level 2:

Friendly samsara travelers within the character's sensory range (excluding the character) gain a 1 DP Morale Bonus on attack checks.

Level 3:

Once per day, the character may spend an hour on a DMR + Expression check, restoring (successes × 1) Willpower to friendly samsara travelers within sensory range (self included); Willpower restored this way caps at the performer's Expression skill. Either way, each unit may recover Willpower through this Feat only once per day.

===

**Barfly (1–3):**

Prerequisites: Socialize 1, with the Information Gathering Profession

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Socialize (Information Gathering) related checks.

Level 2:

In a tavern, nightclub, or similar setting (ST's call), the character's "information gathering" actions take half the total time.

Level 3:

Once per day, in a tavern, nightclub, or similar setting, the character may roll a Socialize (Information Gathering) related check twice and keep the higher successes.

===

**Beautiful Trap (2):**

Prerequisites: City-Toppling Beauty 2, Subterfuge skill 3

Level 2:

The character may substitute DMR for CTR as the key Stat on Subterfuge-related checks.

===

**Devil's Advocate (1–3):**

Prerequisites: CTR 3, Subterfuge skill 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Subterfuge-related checks.

Level 2:

When making a "hasty Bluff check" as one Whole-round Action, the Unnamed Penalty you take is reduced by 3 DP.

Level 3:

Once per day, the character may spend a Free Action and 1 Willpower to make a CTR + Subterfuge (Bluff) check. If it scores more than 5 successes, record all the successful dice; until the Scene changes, you may swap those recorded success dice for failed dice in later rolls. Dice swapped in this way earn no + Dice, at most 5 may be swapped per exchange, and only your own rolls qualify. You may swap dice after a roll ends and its failure is known, changing the outcome.

Special: this Feat cannot affect Opportunity Rolls or Gene Lock opening checks.

===

**Man of a Thousand Faces (1–3):**

Prerequisites: INT 3, Subterfuge skill 3

Level 1:

The character gains a 1 DP Feat Bonus on Subterfuge (Disguise) and Subterfuge (Cover) related checks.

Level 2:

The character's "disguise" and "cover" actions take half the time.

Level 3:

The character gains 1 AS on Subterfuge (Disguise) and Subterfuge (Cover) related checks.

===

**Hear Me Roar (1–3):**

Prerequisites: Intimidation skill 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Intimidation-related checks.

Level 2:

The character may now "Bellow" at targets anywhere within their sensory range — the target must still be able to see the character.

Level 3:

All Profession Bonuses from the character's Intimidation Professions rise to 3, and Intimidation (Bellow) related checks gain 1 AS.

===

**Beast Tamer (1–3):**

Prerequisites: CTR 3, Animal Ken skill 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Animal Ken related checks.

Level 2:

Animals with INT 3 or lower feel an instinctive fondness for you: their attitude rises 1 step, to at most "Warm" (see the "Socialize" skill for attitudes). You may still end up Hostile through botched dealings.

Level 3:

The character may designate one additional unit who can freely command all the character's animal companions. Once per day, the character may change the designated unit as a Free Action.

===

**Master of Humor (nope) (1–3):**

Prerequisites: CTR 3, Socialize skill 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Socialize-related checks.

Level 2:

You may spend a Free Action and 1 Willpower to designate a target within your sensory range: their next check gains the DP Perfect Bonus of Willpower's basic use, as though they had used it themselves — and it counts as their use of Willpower's basic use.

Level 3:

You may spend a Whole-round Action and 3 Willpower, then choose up to (your Legendary CTR + 1) units within 50 meters and make a CTR + Socialize (Negotiation) check, inflicting mental-bind points equal to the successes (non-stacking); targets resist with Will Saves. This is a C-Tier mind-affecting effect.

Special: the character gains this Feat's effects only when the player actually delivers a fitting quip or joke in roleplay. The ST rules on whether the quip/joke qualifies.
