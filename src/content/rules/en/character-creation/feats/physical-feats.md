# Physical Feats

===

**Sense of Direction (1):**

Prerequisites: PCT 2

Level 1:

The character gains a 2 DP Feat Bonus on Survival (Tracking) related checks.

===

**Iron Stomach (1):**

Prerequisites: EDR 3

Level 1:

The character can sample all manner of food without nausea — greasy food while hungover, expired and moldy food, roots and insects in the wilds — though toxins and food poisoning still take effect.

The character gains a 2 DP bonus on checks resisting nausea points, and a 2 DP bonus on checks resisting hunger and thirst.

===

**Cat's Eyes (2):**

Prerequisites: PCT 2; the character lacks the "Impaired Vision" Defect

Level 2: your eyes see in the dark like a cat's from birth.

The character gains low-light vision; visual sensory range increases by 5 meters, with rough visual range increasing in step.

**Rapid Recovery (1–3)**

Prerequisites: EDR 2, Survival skill 2; the character lacks the "Impaired Healing" Defect

Level 1: your wounds mend quickly by nature.

The character's wounds do not deteriorate.

Level 2: your injuries always stabilize quickly.

The character's natural recovery speed doubles.

Level 3: your body never lets wounds corner you.

The character does not fall unconscious for lacking Healthy HP, and Long Rests take half the time.

===

**Wilderness Survival (1–3):**

Prerequisites: Survival skill 3

Level 1: Survival Expert

The craft of staying alive in the wild.

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Survival (Wilderness) related checks.

Level 2: Born to the Wild

Perhaps you suit the wilderness better than the city.

Once per day, when unhappy with a Survival (Wilderness) related check's result, you may reroll it — but you must accept the new result, even if worse.

Level 3: Bear Grylls

Tastes like chicken. Nice and crunchy.

All Profession Bonuses from the character's Survival Professions rise to 3, and Survival (Wilderness) related checks gain 1 AS.

===

**Combat Readiness (1–3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3

Level 1:

The character can draw a carried weapon as a Swift Action, and may fold drawing an arrow from a quiver into any other action without spending the Swift Action.

Level 2:

The character can fold drawing one weapon into a Movement Action at no action cost, or draw two weapons at once as one Swift Action, or stow a weapon (sheathing counts as stowing) as a Movement Action.

Level 3:

The character's hands grow defter still: draw two weapons at once as a Free Action, or fold drawing two weapons into other actions at no cost. The character may also stow weapons as a Swift Action.

Special: this Feat cannot speed the retrieval of objects stored somewhere too concealed or awkward — a backpack, a dimensional pouch (ST's call).

===

**Fleet of Foot (1–3):**

Prerequisites: STR 2

Level 1:

The character's base speed increases by 5 meters.

Level 2:

On charge attacks, the character gains an extra 3 DP Feat Bonus on the attack check.

Level 3:

The character may move as a Swift Action, up to at most (Size × 2) meters.

===

**Initiative Mastery (1–3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 2

Level 1:

The character's Initiative rises by (Feat level × 1).

Level 2:

When another unit ties you on Initiative, you act first; if they hold the same or a similar ability, fall back on the Initiative rules for ordering.

Level 3:

Initiative rises 2 more, and Base Defense rises by 3.

===

**Alertness (1–3):**

Prerequisites: PCT 3, Investigation skill 2

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Investigation-related checks.

Level 2:

While flat-footed, the character retains Base Defense up to this Feat's level.

Level 3:

The character gains D-Tier immunity to being flat-footed.

===

**Nimble Fingers (2–3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 4, Larceny skill 2

Level 2:

The character gains a 3 DP Feat Bonus on Larceny (Theft) related checks.

Level 3:

All Profession Bonuses from the character's Larceny Professions rise to 3, and Larceny (Theft) related checks gain 1 AS.

===

**Strong Back (1–2):**

Prerequisites: STR 2

Level 1:

For carrying capacity, the character's STR counts as higher by this Feat's level.

Level 2:

For judging carried items, the character's Size counts as higher by this Feat's level.

===

**Soundless (1–3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 4, Stealth skill 2

Level 1: Without a Sound

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Stealth-related checks.

Level 2: No Trail in Fallen Leaves

Other units take a 3 DP Feat Penalty on tracking checks against you.

Level 3: Bodiless as Mist

When sneaking without lowering your base speed, your sneaking check loses only 1/4 of its successes.

===

**Endurance (1–3):**

Prerequisites: EDR 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Fortitude Saves against disease and toxins.

Level 2:

EDR checks made while suffocating become Fortitude Saves.

Level 3:

Once per day, pay 1 Willpower to ignore hunger, thirst, or fatigue states for the next Scene.

===

**Racer (1–3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, Drives skill 2

Level 1: Speed Drift

Flashy driving technique.

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Drives-related checks.

Level 2: Straightaway Sprint

Eat my exhaust!

If the character drives a vehicle in a straight-line move, the vehicle may travel an extra 10 meters straight in that move — this may break the vehicle's normal speed cap and is not counted into the vehicle's own speed.

Level 3: Off-Road Rush

Small obstacles might as well not exist.

The Mobility of any vehicle the character drives rises 1 grade; if it is already perfect, the character's Drives-related checks in it gain 1 extra AS. The character's vehicle also ignores difficult terrain.

===

**Stunt Driving (3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 4, Drives skill 3, "Racer" Feat level 1

Level 3:

While driving, the character gains one extra Swift Action per turn, usable only to change the vehicle's direction of movement — see "vehicle movement" in the vehicle rules.

===

**Diehard (1–5):**

Prerequisites: EDR 3, DTR 3

Level 1:

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Fortitude checks.

Level 2:

The character's HP rises by 1, and by 1 more when this Feat reaches level 4.

Level 3:

The character does not lose consciousness from wounds, and gains 1 extra AS on Fortitude checks.

Level 4:

When contesting, saving against, or removing Negative Status points or inherent Negative Statuses from toxins, drugs, disease, or trauma, the character's checks gain a (Feat level) DP Feat Bonus.

Level 5:

The character removes 1 point of Blunt damage per minute; each Long Rest converts Lethal damage equal to EDR back to Healthy. While the character has no "Healthy" HP left, all their checks gain a 1 DP Morale Bonus. Even a health track exactly filled with Aggravated damage does not kill the character — though from then on, even 1 point of Blunt damage will.

===

**Weapon Finesse (2):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3+

Level 2:

On melee Blade attack checks, the character may substitute AGI for STR as the key Stat, gaining a 1 DP Feat Bonus on the check.
