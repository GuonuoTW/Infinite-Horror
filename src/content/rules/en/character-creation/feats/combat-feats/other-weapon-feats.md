# Other Weapon Feats

**Whipping (1–5):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, Blade skill 2 with the "Whip" Profession

Level 1: Whip Shadow

Description: thirty years devoted to the lash — the crack of it startles ghosts and gods.

Blade attacks with **[Flexible Weapon]** arms gain High Speed equal to this Feat's level.

Level 2: The Lash

Description: the whip is the only muscle-powered cold weapon that breaks the sound barrier.

When attacking a target with no Armor Defense using a **[Flexible Weapon]**, gain an extra DP bonus equal to this Feat's level.

Level 3: Entangle

Description: ever seen tentacles?

Grapples or trips made with **[Flexible Weapon]** arms gain an extra DP bonus equal to this Feat's level.

Level 4: The Soft Whip

Description: I won't kill you — I'll just tie you up!

After grappling or tripping an opponent with a **[Flexible Weapon]**, the character may spend a Free Action on a STR contest: each point of margin drives the opponent back 1 meter and knocks them prone; an obstacle behind them deals the remaining meters as Blunt damage. The grapple then ends.

Level 5: Flayed Open

Description: thirty years devoted to the lash — the crack of it startles ghosts and gods.

The character may spend a Standard Action and 1 Willpower to make a Blade attack with a **[Flexible Weapon]** against one target; the attack also inflicts agony points equal to its successes (non-stacking); the target saves with Fortitude.

Special: this Feat may be applied to long, coiling natural weapons — tentacles, say — but your Brawl skill must be level 3, and you need the matching natural weapon's Profession.

**Vehicle Master (2–5)**

Prerequisites: INT 3 or AGI 3, Drives skill 3

You must pilot a vehicle classified "mechanical vehicle" to use and gain all of this Feat's effects.

Level 2: Getting into your own arena fast is where the art begins

The character may now board a vehicle with only a Swift Action; if a large vehicle demands several Movement Actions to board, only one of them downgrades a step.

Level 3: Even a lumbering tractor turns nimble racer in your hands

The Mobility of any vehicle the character pilots rises 1 grade; if already perfect, the character's Drives-related checks in it gain 1 extra AS.

Level 4: The vehicle is home — and stepping into your own home is effortless

Attacks with the vehicle's weapons use the higher of the relevant skill and the driver's Drives skill.

Level 5: Either your mind or your reflexes can fully cover for the other

While piloting, every piloting-related check's key Stat becomes the higher of INT and AGI.

*(Appended breath-attack feat levels:)*

Level 1: your breath-attack checks gain a 3-point Intrinsic Bonus.

Level 2: your breath attacks deal 2 more damage.

Level 3: targets saving against your breath attacks take a (Feat level + 1)-point penalty.

Level 4: your breath checks gain **[9+ Dice]**, and if your breath ability has a "usable again after X rounds" or similar limit, that limit shortens by 1 round.

Level 5: targets cannot use Reflex Actions against your breath attacks.
