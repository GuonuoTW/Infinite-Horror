# Unarmed Feats

**KungFu (1–5):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, Brawl skill 2

You must attack with natural weapons to use and gain all of this Feat's effects.

Level 1: Deft Strikes

Four ounces to move a thousand pounds.

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on melee Brawl attack checks.

Level 2: Iron-Body Qigong

One measure of strength defeats ten of skill.

The character's melee Brawl attacks can deal Lethal damage, and for every AS the character has on the Brawl skill, their natural weapon damage rises 1L.

Level 3: Uncanny Skill

Fighting without nimble strength is like chasing a rabbit with a tractor.

The character may spend a Standard Action and a Swift Action to make one melee Brawl attack plus a Brawl guard; the attack check may substitute AGI for STR.

Level 4: Gale of Blows

Under heaven, only speed cannot be broken.

The character may spend a Standard Action to make two melee Brawl attacks, at different targets or the same one; each takes a 5 DP Unnamed Penalty, and the pair counts as one attack.

Level 5: Concussive Fist

Wa-cha!

The character may spend a Standard Action and 1 Willpower to make a melee Brawl attack against one target, additionally inflicting paralysis points equal to the attack's successes (non-stacking); the target saves with Fortitude. Spending 3 Willpower instead requires no action — it folds into another melee Brawl attack action.

**Qinna Grappling (1–5):**

Prerequisites: STR 3, AGI 3, Brawl 2

Level 1: Grapple Mastery

Quick eyes, quick hands — one grab and done.

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Brawl (Grapple) related checks.

Level 2: Bear Hug

How about a warm embrace?

In grapple contests, the character's Size counts as higher by (this Feat's level × 1).

Level 3: Tendon Splitting

Keeping a man from moving is simpler than you'd think.

When the character attempts to pin a target, the target's STR counts as lower by this Feat's level; if that would drop it to 0 or below, the target cannot resist the pin at all.

Level 4: Throat Lock

However great the strength, it's useless without breath.

If the character pins a target, all the target's speech-based abilities fail automatically — including but not limited to the Song of the Norns and spells with verbal components; abilities being maintained are forced to end, though effects already running that need no upkeep are unaffected. The pinned target also enters "suffocation" until they escape the pin.

Level 5: Bone Grinding

The tendon-splitting, bone-grinding hand!

When attacking a target the character has grappled, the character also inflicts limb-impairment points on the target's limbs equal to the attack's damage, stacking; the target saves with Fortitude.

===

**Sweeping Kick (2):**

Prerequisites: STR 3, Brawl skill 2

Level 2:

Once per round, after dealing at least 1 damage with a melee Brawl attack, the character may designate one damaged unit; that unit must make an AGI + Athletics (Balance) check at DC equal to the damage taken, falling prone on a failure. This Feat has no effect on targets more than 3 Size larger than the character or with STR more than 3 above the character's.

===

**Brawling Expert (3–5)**

Prerequisites: Brawl skill level 3+

Level 3: Wild Flailing

When making Brawl attacks while gripping an improvised weapon in a natural weapon, add the improvised weapon's damage to the natural weapon, up to this Feat's level.

When making Blade attacks with an improvised weapon held in a natural weapon, add the natural weapon's damage to the improvised weapon, up to this Feat's level.

Level 4: Savage Beatdown

Fighting with improvised weapons, the character may wield objects or creatures up to (Legendary STR × 1) Sizes larger than themselves; each point of Size the object or creature exceeds the character adds 1 weapon damage. Carrying-capacity requirements still apply.

Level 5: Instinctive Fighting

While in melee holding an improvised weapon, before taking damage the character may spend a Reflex Action and 3 Willpower to make one Technique-free melee Brawl or melee Blade attack against the attacker within reach.
