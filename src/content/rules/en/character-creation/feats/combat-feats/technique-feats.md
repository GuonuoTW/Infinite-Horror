# Technique Feats

**Arrow Deflection (3):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, PCT 3

Level 3:

The character must have at least one free hand — or a grasping arm-like limb — to use this Feat. When struck by a ranged solid-projectile weapon, the character may make an AGI + PCT check at DC equal to the damage about to be dealt. On a success, the weapon is batted aside and the character takes no damage.

The character must be aware of the attack and not flat-footed. They cannot deflect weapons larger than half their own Size, or with High Speed at or above the lower of their AGI and PCT; the Feat may trigger at most (Legendary PCT + 1) times per round.

===

**Feint Mastery (2):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, INT 3, Subterfuge skill 2

Level 2:

The character may now feint with only a Movement Action, and gains a 1 DP Feat Bonus on feint checks.

===

**Blind-Fighting (4):**

Prerequisites: PCT 4, Investigation skill 3

Level 4: the character gains blindsense out to their own sensory range.

===

**Mounted Combat (1–4):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, Animal Ken skill 3

Level 1: Fighting from the Saddle

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Animal Ken (Riding) related checks.

Level 2: Mounted Combat Mastery

While riding a biological vehicle, the character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on attack checks.

Level 3: Mount Evasion

While riding a biological vehicle, once per turn when attacked, the character may substitute the mount's defense for their own; doing so, the character also gains a 3-point Evade bonus on defense.

Level 4: Furious Charge

On a mounted charge, the character may ignore every object (creatures included) smaller than half the mount's Size that might obstruct the charge.

===

**Nimble Warrior (1–4):**

Prerequisites: AGI 3, PCT 3

The character must wear light armor or none at all to use and gain all of this Feat's benefits.

Level 1: Light as a Swallow

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Athletics-related checks.

Level 2: Swift Fighter

The character's base speed rises by (this Feat's level × 3) meters.

Level 3: Practiced Evasion

The character gains (this Feat's level + 1) Evade Defense on defense.

Level 4: Swift as the Wind God

The character jumps without run-ups and ignores difficult terrain, and gains 1 defense AS.

===

**Heavy Armor Master (1–4):**

Prerequisites: STR 3, EDR 3

The character must wear heavy armor to use and gain all of this Feat's benefits.

Level 1: Heavy Armor Training

While in heavy armor, the character's Armor Penalty drops by (this Feat's level × 1).

Level 2: Heavy Armor Focus

While in heavy armor, the armor's Armor Defense rises by this Feat's level.

Level 3: Heavy Armor Mastery

While in heavy armor, the armor gains anti-Breach value equal to this Feat's level.

Level 4: Lord of Iron

While in heavy armor, the armor gains **[Sealed 1]** — or +1 to its existing **[Sealed]**.

===

**Evasion and Guarding (1–5):**

Prerequisites: AGI 4

Level 1: Deft Guard

When guarding, the character's guard bonus on defense rises 1; and on full defense, their Evade bonus on defense rises 1.

Level 2: Deft Evasion

The character's Base Defense rises by (this Feat's level + 1).

Level 3: Guard Expert

When guarding, the character gains 1 extra AS on defense, counting as guard-derived; on full defense, 1 extra AS on defense, counting as AGI/PCT-derived.

Level 4: Evasion Expert

The character gains a 6-point Evade bonus on defense.

Level 5: Combat Master

The character gains a 3-point Insight Bonus on defense; and while maintaining a guard, 1 point of all-damage absorption. If an attack breaks or ignores all the character's guard bonuses on defense, it also ignores this level's damage absorption.

===

**Defense Within Offense (4):**

Prerequisites: INT 3

Level 4:

On a melee attack check, the character may take an Unnamed Penalty up to (the higher of INT and AGI × 1) on the check, gaining an equal Evade bonus on defense; the bonus is lost after the character takes one attack.

===

**Disarm Mastery (1):**

Prerequisites: none

Level 1:

The character ignores the called-shot penalty for "striking the weapon".

===

**Trip Mastery (3):**

Prerequisites: the "Defense Within Offense" Feat

Level 3:

After successfully tripping a target, the character may spend a Movement Action on one melee attack against it; the attack may attach no Technique or ability, though passives apply as normal.

===

**Killer Physician (1–5)**

Prerequisites: INT 4, Medicine skill 3

Level 1: To Save a Life

The character gains a Feat Bonus equal to this Feat's level on Medicine-related checks.

Level 2: Outshines a Seven-Story Pagoda

The character ignores 3 points of called-shot penalty, and Medicine-related checks gain 1 more Feat Bonus.

Level 3: But Sometimes the Way to Save One Person

After a melee attack, the character may forgo its damage and instead inflict agony Negative Status points equal to the total damage; Fortitude save; non-stacking.

Level 4: Is to Kill Another

When using the level-3 effect, the character may also inflict non-stacking bleeding points; each saves separately with Fortitude.

Level 5: — by a certain revolver-toting quack who just shot his own teammate

The character may spend a Standard Action and 1 Willpower on a melee touch attack against any target — a to-hit check. On a hit, the character makes an INT + Medicine (Anatomy) check: each success upgrades 1 point of damage on the target by one grade (Blunt → Lethal → Aggravated).

*(Appended summoning feat levels:)*

Level 3: Double Summon

You may now summon and control two identical summons rather than one.

Level 4: Three of a Kind

You may now summon and control three identical summons rather than one.

Level 5: Price of Blood

When summoning with an ability, skill, or spell that has a cost, you may instead take unavoidable Lethal damage equal to the cost.

===

**Steel Phantom (1–5)**

Prerequisites: AGI 4, Drives 3

Level 1: Mentor of Inertia

Driving a light vehicle, after a successful acceleration check, the vehicle gains 3 extra Evade Defense until its speed next drops.

Level 2: Vanguard of Gravity

Driving a light vehicle at nonzero speed, vehicle attacks against targets that have not moved this turn gain a 6 DP Speed Bonus.

Level 3: Lord Beyond Limits

While Mentor of Inertia is active, each further successful acceleration check raises the Evade Defense by 2 more, at most twice.

Level 4: Inexhaustible Gale

When your deliberately steered light vehicle lands a successful ram, you may halve the collision damage the obstacle takes and become immune to that damage yourself.

Level 5: Boundless Freedom

While you drive a light vehicle, whenever any enemy unit drives a vehicle toward you, you immediately gain one Movement Action usable only to steer the vehicle — once per round.
